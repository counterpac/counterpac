Secret donors are corrupting our democracy. Thanks to Citizens United, the flow of untraceable money into our elections is getting worse by the day.

Luckily, we know exactly how to get secret money out of an election — and it doesn’t take an act of Congress to do it. It just takes a promise. That's why we're asking the Georgia Senate candidates to take the CounterPAC pledge.

It's easy: Candidates pledge that if any group spends secret money on their behalf, they’ll give money from their own campaign to a charity of their opponent's choice. With an agreement like this in place, suddenly the power of secret donors is gone.
